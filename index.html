Our Heritage of Excellence

Mastering the art of structural construction in Asheboro.

Mason Video Productions, LLC, led by Robert E. Mason, is a premier specialized trade contractor based in Asheboro, NC. With deep expertise in foundation, structure, and building exterior work, we have built a reputation for delivering uncompromising quality. Our team specializes in masonry and other stonework, ensuring that every project reflects both aesthetic beauty and structural permanence. We serve the broader North Carolina region, bringing decades of technical knowledge to residential and non-residential building construction. At our core, we believe that a building is only as strong as its foundation, which is why we approach every job with meticulous attention to detail. Whether it's complex stonework or critical structural reinforcement, we are dedicated to setting the industry standard for craftsmanship.
